Animal Care & Cafe Rules

Our cats always come first. These simple rules help keep everyone safe and happy.

Cafe Rules

Please read these before your visit

1

Keep Noise Down

Please speak quietly and avoid sudden loud noises. It can startle and stress the cats.

2

No Flash Photography

You can take photos but please never use flash. It can hurt the cats eyes and scare them.

3

Let Cats Come to You

Never pick up a cat that has not come to you. Let them decide if they want to be stroked.

4

Do Not Disturb Sleeping Cats

If a cat is sleeping, please leave them alone. They have their own rest areas that are private.

5

Do Not Feed the Cats

Never give cats food from your plate. A lot of human food is harmful to cats.

6

Supervise Children

Children under 12 must be with an adult at all times when in the cat area.

7

No Running or Chasing

Please walk slowly and calmly. Running or chasing cats is not allowed.

8

Watch the Doors

Always check before opening the cat area doors. Our cats must not be allowed to escape outside.

9

Report Any Problems

If a cat looks unwell or is acting strangely, please tell a staff member straight away.

How We Look After Our Cats

We take animal welfare very seriously

1

Regular Vet Check-ups

All our cats see a vet regularly for vaccinations, dental checks and general health checks.

2

Private Rest Areas

Every cat has their own quiet space away from visitors where they can relax whenever they want.

3

Daily Playtime

Our cats get daily enrichment activities like puzzle feeders, climbing frames and toy wands.

4

Healthy Diet

Each cat is fed a vet-approved diet tailored to their age and individual health needs.

5

Quiet Hours

The cafe has set quiet times each day when the cat area is closed so cats can rest in peace.

6

Trained Staff

All of our team have completed animal welfare training and know how to spot signs of stress in cats.

Hygiene Guidelines

Keeping things clean protects both our cats and our visitors

1

Wash Your Hands

Please wash your hands before entering the cat area. Hand sanitiser stations are also available.

2

Cat Allergy Advice

If you have a cat allergy we recommend taking antihistamines before your visit. Visit at your own risk.

3

No Outside Animals

Dogs and other pets are not allowed inside the cafe. This keeps our cats safe and stress-free.

4

If You Are Unwell

Please rebook if you are feeling ill. Some human illnesses can be passed on to cats.

5

Avoid Strong Scents

Please do not wear strong perfume or cologne. Cats have very sensitive noses and it can bother them.

6

Regular Cleaning

The cat area is cleaned twice a day using cat-safe products. Litter trays are checked throughout the day.
